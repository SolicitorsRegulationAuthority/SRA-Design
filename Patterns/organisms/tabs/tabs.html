Guidance on the SRA's approach to financial penalties

We have published guidance to provide a practical approach for decision makers to determine financial penalties which fulfil the requirements of the Financial Penalty Criteria. This guidance applies to fines imposed upon individuals and firms (recognised bodies and licensed bodies). All fines are subject to the limits placed on the SRA's fining powers in law from time to time.
